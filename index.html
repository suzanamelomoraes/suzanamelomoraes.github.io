What's the difference between Padding, Border and Margin?

One of the first things I get struggled to understand with no technology background was the difference between Padding, Border and Margin. Now I know that is that while padding deals with the inner space (the space between an element content and the border), margin deals with the outer space (the space outside of the element) to the next outer element.

Auckland Domain, the city’s oldest park, is based around an extinct volcano and home to the formal Wintergardens. You can find more information on Wikipedia and Aucklandnz.com

Do you know what is a bootcamp?

In Dev Academy you start with a foundations program which one you can choose to complete within 10 weeks studying part-time or full time dedication for 5 weeks. Once completed, you'll join a 9 week experience of intense coding at either in Auckland or Wellington campus. You will be exposed to a variety of modern technologies for full stack web programming.

The best thing about it is that you will graduate with a portfolio of projects that you've designed and built, ready to show to employers.

Linux

My first love when I started to understand a little bit about the development's world was Linux. I soon I got married and tried to use my husband's laptop for the first time I just couldn't. Then I learnt how to use it and fell in love.

Just like Windows, iOS, and Mac OS, Linux is an operating system. In fact, one of the most popular platforms on the planet, Android, is powered by the Linux operating system. An operating system is software that manages all of the hardware resources associated with your desktop or laptop.
